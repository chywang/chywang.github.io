Biography

Chengyu Wang is a third-year Ph.D. student at School of Computer Science and Software Engineering, East China Normal University, supervised by Prof. Xiaofeng He and Prof. Aoying Zhou. He has won National Scholarship FIVE times, awarded by Ministry of Education of PR China. Currently, his research interests mainly focus on knowledge extraction and deep learning techniques for NLP. He has published 20+ research papers in international conferences and journals, such as ACL, WWW, CIKM, EMNLP, etc.
