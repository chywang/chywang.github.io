Biography

Chengyu Wang is an algorithm expert at Alibaba Group. He has obtained his Ph.D degree from East China Normal University (ECNU), supervised by Prof. Xiaofeng He and Prof. Aoying Zhou. Currently, he works on deep learning algorithms on various topics for Alibaba Cloud Machine Learning Platform of AI (PAI) (including natural language processing, human speech recognition and understanding, multi-modality learning, transfer learning, few-shot learning and federated learning), and builds NLP toolkits named EasyTransfer (open-sourced in Tensorflow) and EasyNLP (open-sourced in PyTorch) for Alibaba Cloud. He has published 80+ research papers in international conferences and journals, such as ACL, KDD, WWW, SIGIR, AAAI, TKDE, WSDM, CIKM, SDM, EMNLP, etc.
