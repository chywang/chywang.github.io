Biography

Chengyu Wang is a second-year PhD student at School of Computer Science and Software Engineering, East China Normal University, supervised by Prof. Aoying Zhou and Prof. Xiaofeng He. He has won National Scholarship four times, awarded by Ministry of Education of PR China. Currently, his research interests mainly focus on knowledge extraction and deep learning techniques for NLP. He has published 15+ research papers in international conferences and journals, such as WWW, CIKM, COLING, DASFAA, etc.
