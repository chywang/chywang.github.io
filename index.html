Biography

Chengyu Wang is a final-year Ph.D. student at School of Software Engineering, East China Normal University, supervised by Prof. Xiaofeng He and Prof. Aoying Zhou. He has won National Scholarship FIVE times, awarded by Ministry of Education of PR China. Currently, his research interests mainly focus on knowledge extraction, word semantics and natural language understanding. He has published 30+ research papers in international conferences and journals, such as ACL, WWW, AAAI, TKDE, CIKM, SDM, EMNLP, COLING, KAIS, etc.
